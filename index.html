What is Shuleh?

1

Shuleh is an integrated school management platform that enables education institutions to serve its students and their parents and guardians.

2

It is developed for the school's administration and education to ease the day-to day work of teaching, caring for and students.

3

It's a cloud based system that does not require any capital investment in technology to use. It can be used in the school's current computers.

4

Shuleh is designed to be easy to use, with intuitive processes that do not require any new skills from the users..

5

It is accessible through web portals and mobile apps. It is supported by an integrated payments gateway and SMS messaging platform.

Our Features

Administration

This is the administration’s control platform. It enables the school’s admin and operations team to register new students, assign classes, issue school IDs etc. Also manages teachers, assigns classes and monitors attendance. Can also provide online application processes, allowing students to register for courses.

Learning & Class Management

Enables the school to manage teachers, their classes and the courses. This module enables the issuance and submission of assignments for assessment. It allows the school to publish results and issue transcripts to students. Library modules can also be integrated here.

Communication

This module provides a centralized and integrated channel to send messages to the parents/students via SMS or parents app etc. A digital diary enables teachers to update parents on their children’s progress. Other messaging such as calendars, events & announcements that can be configured.

Revenue Management

This module enables parents to pay fees through mobile money, banks and credit/debit cards. It automatically reconciles payments received to the student’s account. It allows sending of reminders and statements to parents and/or students. A front office feature enables collection of fees at the school.

Key Features

TRACKING

System tracks school bus, its routes, speed etc and notifies admin of any deviations.

AUTOMATED DISMISSAL

Unique IDs can be given to parents/guardians, which must be presented before a child/student is allowed to leave the school.

2 WAY FEEDBACK

Allows parents to respond to teachers’ or school’s communication. Allows surveys and collaboration.

ATTENDANCE REPORTING

Tracks students attendance of classes and co-curricular events. Tracks teachers in class.

ALUMNI or STUDENTS PORTAL

Portal that enables sharing on videos, photos, audio and reconnection with alumni for alerts and announcements

CASHLESS WALLET

Eliminate all cash in school to increase security, reduce bullying and digitize spending.

Why Shuleh?

Agility

Shuleh is customizable to provide a fit-for-purpose solution. This enables quick upgrades, modifications & additions with little distruption to the system.








Scalability

Shuleh can be integrated to any existing system at the school and can be extended to include further modules such as student tracking,school fees payment and reconcilliation, asset tracking (e.g. buses & routes), cashless wallets (for pocket money & events), and e-learning.



Reporting

System provides a centralised dashboard that updates, provides a global view of students, teachers,parents, attendance, school fees payment e.t.c. This dashboard will be able to generate analytical information showing daily, monthly, quartely and annual trends in whichever dataset required by the school.

Knowledge transfer

We will train the relevant school staff to ensure that they can fully operate and manage the platform to provide necessary support. We will also ensure the designated ICT staff are able to respond rapidly to any system troubleshooting requests from teachers or parents.
